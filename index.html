What is DittoShare?

DittoShare is a desktop application that will allow users to eliminate the issue of not being able to watch videos in real-time with friends who are not physically in the same room. The main functions of our application is to be able to join or host rooms for your friends to join, and to be able to share your view of the desktop with them at ease. By sharing your desktop, you will be able to eliminate the barrier of either being a little bit ahead or a little bit behind your friend if you start the video at the “same time”

Why DittoShare?

We wanted to make a free alternative to a former screensharing application, Rabbit, that was unfortunately shut down.
